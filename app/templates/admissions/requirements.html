General Admission Requirements

Prospective students must meet the following general requirements:

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) with at least 5 passes.
  • Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) with at least 2 principal passes.
  • Equivalent qualifications for international students (subject to verification by UNEB).

Program-Specific Requirements

In addition to general requirements, some programs may have specific subject prerequisites or higher grade requirements. Please consult the detailed information for each program you are interested in.

View specific program requirements here.

Required Documents

Typically, you will need to submit certified copies of:

  • Academic transcripts and certificates.
  • National ID or Passport.
  • Birth certificate.
  • Passport-sized photographs.
