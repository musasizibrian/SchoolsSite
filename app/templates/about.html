Our History

Kabale University is a public university located in Kabale, Uganda. Established in 2002, we are committed to providing quality education, research, and community service.

Our university offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality education, promote research and innovation, and contribute to community transformation.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement.
